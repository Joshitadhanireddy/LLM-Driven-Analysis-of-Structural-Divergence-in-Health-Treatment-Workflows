CHLAMYDIA TREATMENT WORKFLOW (from Wikipedia-based plan)

1. Diagnosis Confirmation
   - NAAT test (urine or swab)
   - Assess pregnancy status & coinfections.

2. First‑Line Antibiotic Treatment
   - Doxycycline 100 mg BID x 7 days (more effective in men)
   - Azithromycin 1 g single dose
   - Alternatives: erythromycin, levofloxacin, ofloxacin
   - Pregnancy: erythromycin or amoxicillin

3. Partner Management
   - PDPT (Patient‑Delivered Partner Therapy)
   - Provide medication/prescription to treat partners.

4. Follow‑Up
   - Retest at 3 months (reinfection check)
   - Avoid early test of cure (NAAT can be false‑positive temporarily).
