HEARING LOSS MANAGEMENT WORKFLOW (from Merck-based plan)

1. Treat underlying cause
   - Stop/adjust ototoxic medications; monitor drug levels.
   - Drain middle ear effusions (myringotomy + tympanostomy tube).
   - Remove obstructive benign/malignant lesions.
   - Autoimmune causes → corticosteroids.  fileciteturn13file1

2. Structural repair
   - Tympanoplasty, ossicular reconstruction, stapes surgery when needed.  fileciteturn13file1

3. Amplification options
   - Hearing aids tailored to audiogram.
   - Bone conduction aids or implantable BAHAs for canal obstruction.  fileciteturn13file1

4. Cochlear implants
   - For severe/profound loss with poor hearing-aid benefit.
   - Improves speech understanding and environmental sound detection.  fileciteturn13file1

5. Assistive strategies
   - Captioning, FM/infrared systems, lip-reading/speech-reading training.  fileciteturn13file1
