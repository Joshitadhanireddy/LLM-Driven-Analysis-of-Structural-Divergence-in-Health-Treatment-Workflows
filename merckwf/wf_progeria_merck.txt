PROGERIA (HGPS) MANAGEMENT WORKFLOW (from Merck-based plan)

1. Disease-modifying therapy
   - Lonafarnib: oral FTI that prevents progerin buildup.
   - Shown to reduce mortality significantly (3.7% vs 33.3%).  fileciteturn13file2

2. Monitoring & systemic care
   - Cardiovascular, growth, vision, dental follow-up.
   - Use aspirin, statins, anticoagulants as needed.

3. Functional & supportive care
   - Physical therapy for joint stiffness.
   - High-calorie nutrition; hydration.
   - Hearing and eye care.

4. Goal
   - Slow disease progression; maintain comfort and lifespan.
