TYPE 2 DIABETES MANAGEMENT WORKFLOW (from Merck-based plan)

1. Foundational lifestyle therapy
   - Diet: whole-food, fiber-rich, low–added sugar.
   - Exercise: ≥150 min/week aerobic + resistance.
   - Weight loss emphasized; GLP‑1 agonists/SGLT2 inhibitors preferred for weight benefit.  fileciteturn13file4

2. Pharmacologic therapy
   - First-line: metformin.
   - Add-on:
     * GLP‑1 agonists (effective, weight loss).
     * SGLT2 inhibitors (renal + cardiovascular benefit).
     * TZDs, DPP‑4 inhibitors, sulfonylureas.  fileciteturn13file4

3. Insulin indications
   - A1c >10%, glucose ≥300 mg/dL.
   - DKA/HHS, weight loss, catabolism.
   - Pregnancy.  fileciteturn13file4

4. Complication prevention
   - ACE inhibitors/ARBs, statins.
   - Foot care: daily inspection, podiatry.
   - Vaccinations: pneumococcal, influenza, hepatitis B, varicella, SARS‑CoV‑2.  fileciteturn13file4

5. Education
   - Carb counting, insulin adjustment, recognition of hypo/hyperglycemia.  fileciteturn13file4
