TETANUS TREATMENT WORKFLOW (from Merck-based plan)

1. Core principles
   - Keep patient in quiet environment.
   - Maintain ventilation; intubation/mechanical ventilation if needed.  fileciteturn13file3

2. Wound management
   - Immediate, thorough debridement.
   - Antibiotics (metronidazole preferred).  fileciteturn13file3

3. Neutralize toxin
   - TIG 500 units IM; consider local infiltration.
   - Vaccinate at different site; infection does NOT confer immunity.  fileciteturn13file3

4. Control spasms
   - Benzodiazepines (diazepam, midazolam).
   - Neuromuscular blockade (vecuronium) + ventilation if required.
   - Intrathecal baclofen or dantrolene in refractory cases.  fileciteturn13file3

5. Manage autonomic dysfunction
   - Morphine, clonidine, esmolol.
   - Magnesium infusion with monitoring.  fileciteturn13file3

6. Supportive care
   - Nutrition, bowel/bladder care, chest physiotherapy, turning.  fileciteturn13file3
