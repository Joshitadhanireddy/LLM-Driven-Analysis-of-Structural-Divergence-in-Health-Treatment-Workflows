GERD MANAGEMENT WORKFLOW (from Mayo Clinic-based plan)

1. First-line: lifestyle + OTC meds
   - Antacids (calcium carbonate).
   - H2 blockers (cimetidine, famotidine, nizatidine).
   - Nonprescription PPIs (omeprazole, lansoprazole, esomeprazole).

2. Prescription medications
   - Stronger PPIs (rabeprazole, pantoprazole, dexlansoprazole).
   - Prescription H2 blockers.
   - P-CABs: vonoprazan, tegoprazan for severe reflux.

3. Non-medication therapy
   - Diaphragmatic breathing training post-meals.

4. Surgical/procedural options
   - Fundoplication (Nissen, Toupet).
   - LINX magnetic bead ring.
   - TIF (Transoral incisionless fundoplication).

5. Special consideration
