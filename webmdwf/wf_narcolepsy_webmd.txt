NARCOLEPSY TREATMENT WORKFLOW (from WebMD-based plan)

1. Core principle
   - No cure; treatment reduces sleepiness & cataplexy, improves life quality.  fileciteturn11file3

2. Medications
   2.1 Stimulants for daytime sleepiness:
       - Modafinil, armodafinil.
       - Amphetamines, methylphenidate (older, habit‑forming).  fileciteturn11file3
   2.2 Antidepressants for REM/cataplexy:
       - SNRIs, SSRIs (venlafaxine, fluoxetine, sertraline).
       - Tricyclics (protriptyline, imipramine, clomipramine).  fileciteturn11file3
   2.3 Sodium oxybate (Xyrem, Xywav):
       - Treats cataplexy & improves night sleep.  fileciteturn11file3
   2.4 Wake‑boosting medications:
       - Pitolisant (Wakix), solriamfetol (Sunosi).  fileciteturn11file3

3. Alternative approaches
   - Massage therapy.
   - Meditation‑relaxation to reduce sleep paralysis frequency.  fileciteturn11file3

4. Lifestyle strategies
   - Avoid caffeine, alcohol, nicotine.
   - Regular schedule, small frequent meals.
   - Scheduled 10–15 min naps during day.
   - Exercise 4–5 hours before bedtime.  fileciteturn11file3
