CHRONIC KIDNEY DISEASE (CKD) MANAGEMENT WORKFLOW (from WebMD-based plan)

1. Core kidney‑friendly diet
   - Reduce sodium (<2g/day) to control BP & swelling.  fileciteturn10file1
   - Limit phosphorus (choose low‑phos foods; avoid processed foods).  fileciteturn10file1
   - Reduce potassium if levels high (avoid bananas, potatoes, tomatoes; choose low‑K fruits/veggies).  fileciteturn10file1
   - Adjust protein intake (lower as CKD worsens).  fileciteturn10file1

2. Fluid guidance

   - Track sodium, fluid, potassium, phosphorus.
